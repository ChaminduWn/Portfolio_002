❤️🔥 Ape Mehewara

A project that reflects our commitment to empowering education and uplifting communities. As part of the Leoistic Year 2025/26, I had the privilege of initiating and chairing this project alongside an incredible team whose dedication transformed ideas into meaningful impact. 💪✨

📚 Phase 01: Dhamma School Renovation

📍 Kandē Purāna Maha Vihāraya

Our first phase focused on cleaning and renovating the Dhamma School premises at Kandē Purāna Maha Vihāraya, creating a safer, cleaner, and more comfortable learning environment for students. The project involved restoring classroom furniture, improving the surrounding facilities, and encouraging community participation in supporting educational and religious institutions. Through these efforts, we were able to enhance the learning experience of more than 1,200 students. 🙏📖

➗ Phase 02: Mathematics Laboratory

📍 Royal College, Horana

The second phase focused on transforming an underutilized classroom at Royal College, Horana into a fully functional Mathematics Laboratory. By improving infrastructure and providing essential educational resources, we created an engaging space that encourages practical learning and academic growth. The project not only benefited students but also supported teachers in delivering a more interactive learning experience while promoting sustainable educational development. 🎓📐

🌟 "Ape Mehewara was more than a project, it was a commitment to creating opportunities, strengthening educational foundations, and serving communities with purpose. ❤️"
