2024 – 2025 · Leo Club of SLIIT

👣 Born To Roam ... 🤍💫

A name that echoes our mission: to protect wildlife and life below water. As part of the Leoistic year 2024/25, I had the immense honor of chairing this project with great passion. It wasn't a solo journey — it was a collaboration filled with dedication, unity, and unwavering support from my fellow Leo members & My Team 💪.

🐢 Phase 01: Turtle Conservation

📍 Hikkaduwa Turtle center & Coastal Area (2024.11.24)

We began with a turtle conservation project in Hikkaduwa, feeding and cleaning tanks to improve their habitat. We also created a vibrant mural to share the story of turtle conservation — turning art into advocacy. 🎨🌊

🐝 Phase 02: Bee Conservation

📍 Mirigama (2025.02.19)

To tackle the decline of honey bees, we hosted a bookmark fundraiser that raised Rs. 40,000+, enabling us to donate 10 bee boxes to elderly individuals. This supported pollinators and empowered communities through sustainable honey production. 🌼🍯

🌊 Phase 03: Coral Replantation

📍 Tangalle (2025.05.03)

Partnering with marine biologist Mr. Chathushka Nadeniya, we led a coral replantation initiative. Cement blocks embedded with coral fragments were placed underwater to revive reefs, enhance biodiversity, and protect our coasts. 🪸🐠

💫 "Born To Roam was more than a project — it was a movement driven by teamwork, perseverance, and love for nature. I'm grateful to every member who helped bring this vision to life."

Let's keep roaming, learning, and protecting — for a better tomorrow.🌿🌎
